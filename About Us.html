OUR STORY

With a team coming from art school, we know that we have to buy a lot of expensive art materials, which we probably use only a few times for one project and never touch them again. The art supplies might stay on the shelf and covered in dust, while it could be used to create amazing art by other creatives. With that thought in mind, I create this service called The Artpothecary to give all artists a resource to sell and buy affordable art supplies, with the hope that we could make art accessible for everyone.
